About Us

We are a creative server that strives to fufil one promise - to have the best, most realistic, highest quality builds that have ever been made in Minecraft.

Since our creation in 2015, Blueprint Realism has grown into a thriving community, partnered with three? other servers. With projects ranging from the World Trade Centre and the White House, to server-wide creations such as Anatora and Idaho, we have attempted what was never been done before.

At the moment, we have three main projects! Nations, run by Wessaux, and also two World Trade Centres, one by Jimphf, and the other by MrSquishyMan.

We welcome you to join today and see for yourself.
